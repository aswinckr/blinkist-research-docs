### Strong Evidence For:

1. **Organic Adoption** — CS-001 (HAYS), CS-002 (Amazon) show self-directed engagement
2. **Hybrid Learning Value** — CS-006 (AMBOSS) validates self-paced + live + coaching
3. **Mobile/Audio Fit** — CS-008 (Matillion) confirms learning outside office hours

1. **Measurable ROI** — CS-003 (Perlego) provides NPS metric, but only one data point
2. **Behavior Change** — CS-007 (Trustpilot) mentions skills application, no metrics
3. **Leadership Development** — CS-003, CS-005 show leadership use cases

1. **Cost Savings** — No ROI or cost reduction data in any case study

3. **Long-Term Retention** — No multi-year retention data
