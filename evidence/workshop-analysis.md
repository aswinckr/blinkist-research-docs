## 3. Strategic Context Deep-Dive

### The Core Problem Statement

**Primary Threat:** AI commoditization of learning content

> "AI is rapidly commoditizing learning content, summaries, and personalization. Many new players are competing primarily on AI features."

**Evidence of Threat:** [E-WS-01]
- AI-generated summaries now available from multiple providers
- Personalization is table-stakes, not differentiator
- New entrants (unnamed) competing on AI features

### The 5 Human Blockers Framework

A key framework introduced in the workshop proposal:

| # | Blocker | Definition | AI Capability | Human Advantage |
|---|---------|------------|---------------|-----------------|
| 1 | **Motivation** | "Why should I learn this?" | LOW — AI can recommend, can't inspire | HIGH — Human connection drives "why" |
| 2 | **Focus** | "Can I stay engaged?" | MEDIUM — AI can nudge, gamify | MEDIUM — Coaches can intervene |
| 3 | **Accountability** | "Who checks if I follow through?" | LOW — AI reminders are ignorable | HIGH — Humans hold humans accountable |
| 4 | **Application** | "How do I use this at work?" | LOW — AI lacks context | HIGH — Coaches know real situations |
| 5 | **Behavior Change** | "Will this stick?" | LOW — AI tracks, doesn't sustain | HIGH — Habits need reinforcement |

**Strategic Implication:** [E-WS-02] AI solves content access; humans solve learning effectiveness. This is the workshop's central thesis.

### Key Questions by Phase

**Phase 1 — Core Strategic Choice:**
- Compete on AI feature velocity? OR
- Differentiate through human-led learning outcomes?

**Phase 2 — Problem Mapping:**
- Where does learning fail even with strong content and AI?
- Focus: Motivation gaps, focus problems, application challenges, accountability voids

**Phase 3 — Strategy Definition:**
For each option, define:
- Role of AI
- Role of humans
- What to double down on
- What to explicitly deprioritize

**Phase 4 — Bet Selection:**
- Compare strategy options
- Surface key risks and assumptions
- Agree on 1-2 near-term experiments
