## Evidence Strength Key

| Rating | Meaning | Criteria |
|--------|---------|----------|
| **HIGH** | Strong evidence | Multiple sources, verified externally, clear methodology |
| **MEDIUM** | Moderate evidence | Single source, internal data, plausible but unverified |
| **LOW** | Weak evidence | No source cited, methodology unclear, potentially conflated |
| **NONE** | No evidence | Aspirational statement, no supporting data found |
