## Executive Summary

This positioning map plots 10 L&D competitors on two strategic dimensions:
1. **Y-Axis: Market Focus** — Pure SMB/Mid-Market vs. Pure Enterprise
2. **X-Axis: Product Complexity** — Simple/Fast vs. Complex/Feature-Rich

**Key Finding:** There is a significant **Mid-Market gap** — most competitors are either SMB-friendly (simple, low-cost) or Enterprise-focused (complex, high-cost). Blinkist can own the "Simple + Mid-Market" position that currently has no dominant player.
