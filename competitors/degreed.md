## Product Suite

Degreed offers a modular, enterprise-focused product portfolio:

### 1. Degreed Learning (LXP) — Core Platform
**Evidence ID:** COMP-DG-001

The flagship LXP enabling workforce skill development:
- **800+ integrations** including Slack, Microsoft Teams [COMP-DG-002]
- **AI-powered personalization** — automated learning recommendations
- **Plans and Pathways** — curated content curation tools
- **No-code automations** — streamline workflows without development
- **Advanced Analytics** — measure business impact of learning
- **Mobile app** — iOS and Android with offline access

### 2. Degreed Skills+ — Skills Intelligence
**Evidence ID:** COMP-DG-003

Advanced skills data orchestration:
- Skill data normalization across ecosystem
- Custom taxonomy and skill scales
- Skill supply and gap analysis
- Skill inference for incomplete profiles
- Industry data for workforce forecasting
- Skill-based learning personalization

### 3. Degreed Maestro — AI Engine
**Evidence ID:** COMP-DG-004

The AI layer powering the platform:
- AI-driven learning efficiency
- Intelligent content recommendations
- Skill-to-learning matching
- Automation and insights

### 4. Degreed Academies — Cohort Learning
**Evidence ID:** COMP-DG-005

Structured, cohort-based deep skill development:
- Time-bound learning with due dates
- Prerequisites and learning sequences
- Project assignments with feedback
- Peer-to-peer discussions
- Live and async virtual training
- Community development

### 5. Degreed Open Library — Content
**Evidence ID:** COMP-DG-006

Pre-curated learning pathways:
- AI-curated content paths
- Ready-to-deploy learning journeys
- Simplifies content curation burden

### 6. Degreed Professional Services
**Evidence ID:** COMP-DG-007

Expert consulting support:
- Implementation
- Change management
- Skills activation
- Engagement and adoption
- Learning experience design
- Technical consulting

## Strategic Vulnerabilities

**Evidence ID:** COMP-DG-022

### 1. UX Complexity
- Multiple negative reviews cite navigation and discoverability issues
- Enterprise buyers may be frustrated with implementation complexity
- **Blinkist Opportunity:** Emphasize focused, simple UX

### 2. Content Overload
- "Too much content" cited as problem
- Users struggle with content duplication
- **Blinkist Opportunity:** Curated quality over quantity

### 3. Human Touch Gap
- Platform focused on AI + content aggregation
- Lacks built-in coaching or human facilitation
- Opportunities section just "makes them visible" — no matching
- **Blinkist Opportunity:** Human-led activation as differentiator

### 4. Premium Pricing
- Perceived as expensive ($$$$ on G2)
- Professional Services add to total cost
- **Blinkist Opportunity:** Accessible price point

### 5. LXP Not LMS
- Some organizations need LMS compliance features
- "Management capabilities (the M in LMS) are limited"
- **Blinkist Opportunity:** Focus on different use case
