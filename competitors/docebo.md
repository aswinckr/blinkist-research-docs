## Competitive Battlecard

### Head-to-Head Positioning

| Dimension | Docebo | Blinkist Business |
|-----------|--------|-------------------|
| **Primary focus** | Enterprise LMS with skills add-on | Learning for behavior change |
| **AI strategy** | AI-powered LMS features | AI + Human coaching hybrid |
| **Content model** | Third-party marketplace | Original curated summaries |
| **Pricing model** | Custom enterprise quotes | Transparent per-seat |
| **Complexity** | 10+ product modules | Simple, focused |
| **Time-to-value** | Enterprise implementation | Quick deployment |
| **Target buyer** | L&D Directors, CIO | L&D Directors, CHRO |

### Win Scenarios
1. **Mid-market deals** — Docebo's complexity and lack of transparent pricing creates friction
2. **Speed-to-value** — When buyers need quick wins vs. long enterprise implementations
3. **Content quality** — When original, curated content beats generic marketplaces
4. **Human touch** — When AI-only coaching isn't enough for real behavior change
5. **Budget-conscious** — Transparent pricing vs. custom quotes
6. **Simplicity** — When buyers are overwhelmed by complex LMS platforms

### Lose Scenarios
1. **Extended enterprise** — When training customers/partners is key requirement
2. **Government/compliance** — When FedRAMP authorization is required
3. **Skills mapping** — When comprehensive skills intelligence is priority (365Talents)
4. **Existing Docebo customers** — Switching costs are high
5. **eCommerce** — When selling training externally is a requirement

## Strategic Implications for Blinkist

### Opportunities
1. **Market timing** — Docebo's declining metrics and PE acquisition create uncertainty; Blinkist can position as stable alternative
2. **Mid-market gap** — Docebo focuses on enterprise; mid-market underserved
3. **Human differentiation** — Docebo's "Virtual Coaching" is AI-only; Blinkist's human coaching is differentiator
4. **Content quality** — Docebo relies on third-party marketplace; Blinkist's original summaries are unique
5. **Simplicity narrative** — "10+ modules" complexity vs. Blinkist's focused approach

### Threats
1. **365Talents acquisition** — Skills intelligence is the future; Docebo is ahead here
2. **AI investment** — Three AI products already shipped (Harmony, Virtual Coaching, Content Creator)
3. **Enterprise lock-in** — Fortune 500 relationships are sticky
4. **Comprehensive platform** — When buyers want all-in-one, Docebo wins

### Recommended Counter-Positioning
- **Against "AI workforce readiness"** → Position as "AI + Human learning activation"
- **Against 365Talents skills** → Focus on application and behavior change, not just skills mapping
- **Against complexity** → "Get started in days, not months"
- **Against enterprise focus** → Champion the underserved mid-market
