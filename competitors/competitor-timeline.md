**What happened:** The two largest MOOC platforms announced a $2.5B all-stock merger in December 2025, expected to close H2 2026.

**Why it matters for Blinkist:**
- Combined entity will have 40,000+ courses â€” dwarfs every other content library
- Stated goal: "accelerate AI-powered product roadmap" â€” AI investment will increase
- Enterprise focus intensifies â€” both have B2B arms
