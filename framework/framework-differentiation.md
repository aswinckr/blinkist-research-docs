## The Differentiation Framework

### Why Framework-Based Differentiation Matters

Traditional L&D platform differentiation focuses on:
- Content library size (commoditized)
- AI features (commoditized)
- Price point (race to bottom)
- Integrations (catch-up game)

**Framework-based differentiation focuses on:**
- Which stages of learning do you uniquely serve?
- Where is human involvement essential?
- What outcomes can you prove?

This creates defensible positioning because it's based on **structural capability**, not feature lists.
