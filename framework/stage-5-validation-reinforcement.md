## The Reinforcement Challenge

> **"Most corporate learning creates a spike, not a sustained change."** [E-LDT-12]

- Within 1 hour: 50% forgotten
- Within 24 hours: 70% forgotten
- Within 1 week: 80% forgotten
- Within 1 month: 90% forgotten
