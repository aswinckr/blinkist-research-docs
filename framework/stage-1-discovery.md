## Stage Definition

> **Core Question:** "What do I need to learn?"

Discovery is the gateway stage where learners recognize they have a learning need and find relevant content to address it. This stage encompasses both **pull discovery** (learner seeks content) and **push discovery** (content finds learner).

### What Success Looks Like

| Dimension | Failed Discovery | Successful Discovery |
|-----------|-----------------|---------------------|
| **Relevance** | Generic content pushed by L&D | Right content finds learner at right moment |
| **Timeliness** | Weeks/months after need emerges | Just-in-time, facing actual challenge |
| **Awareness** | "I didn't know we had this" | "I knew exactly where to look" |
| **Signal Quality** | Noise (too much, wrong content) | Signal (curated, contextual) |
| **Friction** | 10+ clicks, multiple logins, abandoned | 2-3 clicks, seamless, immediate value |

### Discovery is Commoditized â€” Moat Must Come Elsewhere

**Critical Insight:** [COMP-AI-001]
> AI-powered discovery is 100% commoditized. 10/10 competitors have equivalent or superior AI recommendations. Blinkist cannot differentiate on discovery alone.

### The Curation Advantage is Real but Narrow

**Evidence:** [CF-001] 93% dissatisfied with AI-curated content
**Evidence:** [E-LDT-04] "Information-focused learning is dying"

Blinkist's human curation creates genuine value:
- Quality over quantity
- Avoids content overload
- Trusted source

But this advantage is:
- Hard to prove (no A/B against competitors)
- Invisible until experienced
- Doesn't address downstream stages

### Integration Gap is Strategic Vulnerability

**Evidence:** [COMP-INT-001] Blinkist ~10 integrations vs Degreed 800+
**Evidence:** [GAP-01] "Flow of work" positioning is FATAL contradiction

Blinkist CANNOT credibly claim "learning in flow of work" with current integration depth. Two options:

1. **Build Integrations (18-24 months):** Partner with LMS/LXP platforms, build Slack/Teams embedding
2. **Reframe Positioning (Immediate):** Shift from "flow of work" to "learning that fits your life"

**Recommendation:** Option 2 now, Option 1 later.
