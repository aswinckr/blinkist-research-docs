## Executive Summary

This matrix prioritizes all identified gaps using a 2-dimensional framework:
- **Impact** (1-5): Revenue/conversion cost if gap remains unfixed
- **Fixability** (1-5): Effort/cost/time to resolve the gap

**Prioritization Formula:** Priority Score = Impact × (6 - Fixability)
- Higher score = higher priority
- This weights toward high-impact gaps that are also fixable

## Section 7: Score Calculation Details

### Formula
```
Priority Score = Impact (1-5) × (6 - Fixability (1-5))
```

This formula ensures:
- High-impact, easy-to-fix gaps score highest (25 max)
- High-impact, hard-to-fix gaps score moderately (5-20)
- Low-impact gaps score low regardless of fixability (1-10)

### Score Thresholds

| Score Range | Priority | Rationale |
|-------------|----------|-----------|
| 20-25 | P0 | High impact, achievable quickly |
| 12-19 | P1 | Significant impact, moderate effort |
| 8-11 | P2 | Medium impact or high effort |
| 1-7 | P3 | Low impact or optional |
