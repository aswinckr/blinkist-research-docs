## Section 3: Messaging Reframes (Days 3-10)

These require marketing/positioning decisions but can be executed quickly.

### QW-008: Reframe "Flow of Work" Positioning ⭐⭐

**The Problem:**
Core tagline "Learning shows up where people need it most" (CLM-OUT-001) is **fatally contradicted** by:
- STAT-011: 67% of learning happens outside 9-5 hours
- CS-008: Usage contexts include commuting, pet walking, weekends
- COMP-INT-012: Blinkist has ~10 integrations vs. competitors' 50-800+

**The Fix:**
1. Draft alternative positioning that aligns with ACTUAL usage:
   - FROM: "Learning shows up where people need it most"
   - TO: "Learning that fits your life" OR "Learning for the moments between" OR "The 15-minute business education"
2. Update slides 1, 5, 6 with new messaging
3. Create sales talking points for objection handling
4. Keep flow-of-work as ASPIRATION in roadmap, not current reality

**Evidence Chain:**
- GAP-01: Flow-of-work FATAL gap
