## Executive Summary

**The Claim:** "Built-in analytics track engagement, skill application, and business impact" ‚Äî Blinkist helps L&D leaders prove value and protect their budgets.

**The Reality:** Analytics track *engagement* (content consumption, completion), but there is **no evidence** of mechanisms to track actual *business impact* (behavior change, productivity, revenue contribution).

**The Verdict:** üî¥ **CRITICAL GAP** ‚Äî Core buyer need unmet. 57% of L&D leaders face mounting ROI pressure; Blinkist's analytics prove consumption, not value creation.

**Strategic Implication:** Either BUILD business impact measurement capabilities OR reframe positioning to "engagement leader" while pursuing human coaching for transformational proof.
