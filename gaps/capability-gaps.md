- Reality: 67% of usage is OUTSIDE 9-5 (STAT-011)
- Reality: Usage is commuting, weekends, with pets (CS-008)

**Why this matters:**
- Flow-of-work is THE 2024-2025 L&D buzzword
- Go1's Morgan directly challenges this positioning
- Arist is a funded startup focused entirely on this space

**Blinkist impact:**
- Cannot credibly claim flow-of-work positioning
- Core tagline is contradicted by evidence
- Loses to competitors actually embedding in workflows

**Recommended response:** Reframe positioning to "learning that fits your life" (honest) rather than "learning in flow of work" (contradicted)
