## Recommendations

### 1. Positioning Pivot Required
The evidence overwhelmingly suggests Blinkist should **pivot from AI-centric to Human+AI positioning**:
- AI claims are contradicted by market evidence (commoditization)
- Human coaching is the genuine white space (0/10 competitors offer it)
- Workshop materials validate this strategic direction

### 2. Methodology Documentation is Quick Win
The 72% implementation claim is a critical ROI proof that simply needs methodology documentation. This is a LOW effort, HIGH impact fix that should happen immediately.

### 3. Accept and Disclose Mode Maturity
Rather than implying three equally mature modes, acknowledge:
- On-demand is mature and dominant (96% audio)
- Guided is growing
- Coached is emerging/new

Honesty builds trust and sets appropriate buyer expectations.

### 4. Diversify Case Study Portfolio
The Perlego case is excellent but carries single-source risk. Priority:
- 2-3 additional coaching outcome cases
- 1 enterprise-scale case with metrics
- 1 ROI/cost-savings case
