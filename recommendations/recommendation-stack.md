Based on 8 hours of comprehensive research across evidence foundations, competitor intelligence, market analysis, gap analysis, and strategic framework development, these are the **Top 10 prioritized recommendations** for Blinkist's B2B enterprise strategy.

## Recommendation 5: Create Tiered Product Structure

### Recommendation
**Build clear 4-tier product structure: Self-Serve → Guided → Coached → Certified. This serves both cost-conscious and outcome-focused buyers.**

### Evidence Chain
```
Why Tiers Matter:
├── Current state: Pricing confuses buyers (€83 → €13 unclear)
├── [COMP-PM-011]: Only 4/10 competitors have transparent pricing → advantage
├── Different buyers want different things:
│   ├── Procurement: Cost efficiency (Self-Serve)
│   └── L&D Leaders: Outcomes (Coached/Certified)
└── Upsell path: Land with AI, expand with human

Proposed Tiers:
├── Self-Serve: AI content only, €13-20/seat/year, 80% margin
├── Guided: AI + group webinars (1:many), €40-60/seat/year, 70% margin
├── Coached: AI + cohort coaching (1:20-50), €100-150/seat/year, 60% margin
└── Certified: AI + 1:1 coaching + certification, €300-500/seat/year, 50% margin

Precedent:
├── [PREC-GE-010]: Guild has Guild Talent Advantage + Guild Grow → tiered
├── [COMP-CB-003]: Coursera has Team/Enterprise tiers → works in market
└── [COMP-UB-003]: Udemy has Team/Enterprise/Enterprise Pro → validated
```
