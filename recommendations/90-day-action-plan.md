## Executive Summary

This 90-day plan transforms Blinkist's B2B strategy from AI-first positioning to Human-Powered, AI-Enhanced. The plan prioritizes quick wins in Weeks 1-4, medium bets in Weeks 5-8, and strategic foundations in Weeks 9-13.

### Guiding Principles
1. **Quick wins first** — Build momentum and credibility before major investments
2. **Evidence before expansion** — Document methodology, gather case studies
3. **Human as hero** — Every action reinforces Human + AI positioning
4. **Parallel workstreams** — Marketing, Product, and Sales move together
