## Executive Summary

This matrix synthesizes buyer priorities across three key personas (L&D Director, CHRO/VP People, CEO SMB) into a comprehensive ranking of decision criteria for enterprise learning platform selection. The analysis reveals **security and integration** as the #1 priority for buyers, followed by **measurable ROI** and **AI-powered personalization**.

**Key Finding:** While AI is expected by buyers, it's now table stakes ‚Äî the true differentiators are **human elements, ease of implementation, and demonstrable business impact**. [DC-001]
