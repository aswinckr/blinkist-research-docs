## Executive Summary

This analysis synthesizes L&D budget allocation trends from multiple authoritative sources including ATD's State of the Industry, Training Magazine's Industry Report, and market research firms. Key finding: Despite market volatility, L&D remains a strategic priority with 2024 marking a five-year high in revenue-to-training investment ratios.

**Key Metrics (2024-2025):**
- Total U.S. training expenditure: **$98B (2024)** → **$102.8B (2025)** (+4.9%)
- Per-employee direct spend: **$1,254 (2024)**, down from $1,283 (2023)
- Per-learner spend: **$874 (2025)**, up from $774 (2024)
- Revenue allocation: **2.9% of revenue** to talent development (five-year high)
- Training hours: **40 hours/employee (2025)**, down from 47 (2024)
